# Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 11, 2026

Your privacy is important to us. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

## 1. Information We Collect
- **Authentication Data:** When you log in via the CLI, we store your username and a hashed version of your token.
- **Package Data:** When you publish a package, we store your username as the author.
- **Analytics:** We collect anonymous download statistics to provide the "Most Downloaded" feature.

## 2. How We Use Data
- To provide the package registry service.
- To attribute package ownership.
- To improve the search and discovery of packages.

## 3. Data Storage
Our database is hosted on Neon (PostgreSQL) and files are stored on Cloudflare R2. We use industry-standard security measures to protect your data.

## 4. Third Parties
We do not sell your personal data. We use service providers (like Cloudflare and Vercel) only to the extent necessary to provide the service.

## 5. Your Rights
You can request the deletion of your account and your published packages by contacting us on GitHub.

## 6. Contact
If you have questions, please open an issue on our GitHub repository.
